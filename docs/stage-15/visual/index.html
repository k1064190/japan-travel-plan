Stage 15

Reservation checklist panel tracking 14 bookable items (12 paid admissions + 2 Rapit transit segments) with localStorage persistence, priority sorting, and a progress bar.

Reservation Checklist

Why

Through Stage 14, information display, curation, the map, and the UI were complete. The remaining real user action before departure is booking: USJ tickets, Nankai Rapit, Osaka Castle, Kiyomizudera, Harukas 300, Kaiyukan, Dotonbori River Cruise, and others are cheaper and faster to enter when purchased as e-tickets in advance. Hotels were already booked separately; restaurant queuing is out of scope. A dedicated checklist panel lets the user track booking progress and persist it across sessions.

What

  • Schema: optional place.booking object (required, url, advance_days, ticket_price_jpy, notes) and optional transit_from_prev.booking_url string
  • 12 places given booking: USJ, Osaka Castle, Harukas 300, Kaiyukan, Tonbori River Cruise, Tsutenkaku, Kiyomizudera, Byodoin, Kodaiji, Entokuin, Gion Corner, Fushimi Sake Museum
  • 2 transit segments given booking_url: Day 1 Rapit KIX→Namba, Day 5 Rapit Namba→KIX
  • renderBooking(place, color): “Booking” section in the detail panel between Activities and Restaurants; required/optional badge, price, advance days, notes
  • Transit card inline “e-Ticket →” button
  • #checklist aside panel (desktop) + 3rd mobile tab: 14 cards sorted incomplete > complete, required > optional, earlier advance days first; completed items shown with strikethrough and grey styling
  • Progress bar: “Required N/M complete”; desktop header toggle button “Reservations X/Y”
  • localStorage["reservations"] JSON object for persistence; setReservationDone(id, bool) helper
  • 8 required items: USJ, Osaka Castle, Harukas 300, Kaiyukan, Byodoin, Gion Corner + 2 Rapit segments; 6 optional items for informational display

How

Data curated by 4 parallel subagents (Group 1: USJ + Rapit KIX↔Namba via KKday; Group 2: 5 Osaka attractions; Group 3: 6 Kyoto attractions; Group 4: cross-check verifier). Each group saved a patch JSON to /tmp/booking-patches/; /tmp/merge-bookings.mjs applied all patches with http→https normalization. Group 3 found and corrected outdated prices (Byodoin 600 ¥→700 ¥, Kodaiji+Entokuin combined 900 ¥→1,200 ¥). Validator extended with validateBooking helper following the validateCuratedLink pattern; 4 new test cases added (11→15 passing). collectReservationItems() collects booking entries from both places and itinerary, then sorts them.

Retrospective

The 4-subagent parallel curation pattern (same as Stages 11/12) completed 14 items in ~16 minutes; a Group 4 verifier cross-checked results finding 0 corrections. The optional-only schema additions produced zero regressions across all 169 places. Group 3 caught two outdated ticket prices from the plan document, suggesting price-verification should be a first step in any future data work.
